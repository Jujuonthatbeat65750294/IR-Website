CIEL Textile

CIEL Textile is a member of CIEL Group. We are one of the largest manufacturing industry, with a strong presence in 4 countries, Madagascar, Mauritius, Bangladesh and India and 19 locations. CIEL Textile is comprised of several business units (BUs) namely: Aquarelle Clothing Ltd, Laguna Clothing (Mtius) Ltd, Tropic Knits Ltd, CDL Knits Ltd, Floreal International Ltd and Ferney Spinning Mills. Our main products are sweaters, shirts & T-shirts and the brands we collaborate with are Lacoste, ASOS, Zadig & Voltaire and Tommy Hilfiger amongst others. CIEL Textile is a big ambassador for sustainability and it is reflected in this motto: "We're sustainable, not because we have to, but because we want to". We started our sustainability journey way before it became a trendy word. We believe in our people and have always put them first. Today we consider ourselves as being the best global fashion partner for our clients and we're on a mission to improve fashion. We're also on a mission to find the brightest and most passionate talents. Our workforce is made up of more than 23,000 talents and we are on an ongoing mission to find the best.
